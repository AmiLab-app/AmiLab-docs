# Amigurumi 101

Amigurumi (あみぐるみ) is the Japanese craft of crocheting small, stuffed creatures and objects. Shapes are worked **in the round** — a continuous spiral or joined rounds — starting from a **magic ring**.

Key characteristics:

- **Tight tension** — stitches are worked at a tighter gauge than garment crochet to prevent stuffing from showing
- **Invisible decreases** — decreases are worked through the front loops only to minimize gaps
- **Piece assembly** — arms, legs, heads, and bodies are crocheted separately and sewn together
