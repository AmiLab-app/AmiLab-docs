# Glossary

Short definitions of the terms used across AmiLab and these docs. Where a term has a fuller treatment, a link points to it.

### Base stitch

The stitch (or stitches) in the round below that a stitch is worked into. A stitch with no base starts the piece; a stitch with two or more bases is a decrease. See [how AmiLab models a piece](../explanations/how-amilab-models-a-piece.md).

### Closure

A virtual thread that pulls two stitches together, used to finish a piece — for example, the gathering thread that draws the top of a sphere closed.

### Composition

An assembly of multiple finished pieces positioned together to form one character or object.

### Control point

A point on a [profile](#profile) silhouette that sets how wide the shape is (its radius) at a given height. See [profiles and cross-sections](../explanations/profiles-and-cross-sections.md).

### Cross-section

The shape AmiLab sweeps a profile's silhouette around — circle, circle +, hexagon, or square — which determines the 3D form. See [profiles and cross-sections](../explanations/profiles-and-cross-sections.md).

### Decrease (`dec`)

One stitch worked over two or more bases, reducing the stitch count of a round.

### Default color

The yarn color applied to every stitch that has no color of its own. Setting it recolors the whole piece. See [the color model](../explanations/the-color-model.md).

### Gauge

How many stitches and rows fit in 10 cm of fabric. AmiLab uses it to convert stitch counts into real-world size. See [gauge and dimensions](../explanations/gauge-and-dimensions.md).

### Increase (`inc`)

Two stitches worked into the same base, adding one to a round's stitch count.

### Materialize

To generate a complete stitch pattern from a shape and a few parameters. See [materializing shapes](../explanations/materializing-shapes.md).

### Palette

A small set of colors saved on a project for one-click reuse in the color pickers. See [the color model](../explanations/the-color-model.md).

### Pattern

A single crochet piece — the stitches and rounds you design in the editor.

### Profile

A reusable silhouette designed in the Shapes view, defined by [control points](#control-point) and a [cross-section](#cross-section), that can be swept into a 3D form and materialized as a pattern. See [profiles and cross-sections](../explanations/profiles-and-cross-sections.md).

### Project

A group of related patterns that share a gauge and a color palette.

### Round

A loop of stitches worked around the piece. In AmiLab, rounds are *derived* from the stitch connections rather than stored.

### Working mode

How a piece begins and is built — magic ring, open round, or foundation chain — which determines its starting stitches.
