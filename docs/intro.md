# Introduction

**AmiLab** is a browser-based tool for designing amigurumi crochet patterns with a live 3D preview. Describe your shape — a sphere, tube, or custom profile — and AmiLab generates round-by-round stitch instructions you can take straight to your hook.

## What is amigurumi?

Amigurumi (あみぐるみ) is the Japanese art of crocheting small stuffed animals and characters. Shapes are worked in continuous rounds, starting from a magic ring, and built up stitch by stitch.

## What does AmiLab do?

- **Shape editor** — draw a profile curve that defines your 3D shape
- **Live 3D preview** — see the shape rendered in real time as you edit
- **Pattern output** — generates a round-by-round stitch count in your language and notation
- **Multi-language** — supports US English, UK English, German, Dutch, Japanese, Portuguese, and Chinese stitch notation

## Who is it for?

AmiLab is for crocheters of all skill levels who want to design original amigurumi shapes rather than following someone else's pattern. If you can read a basic stitch count, you can use AmiLab.
