# Set Gauge and Language

Configure your project before you start designing.

## Set Your Gauge

Your gauge tells AmiLab how many stitches fit in 10 cm with your yarn and hook.

1. Open **Settings → Gauge**
2. Enter your stitch gauge (stitches per 10 cm)
3. Enter your row gauge (rows per 10 cm)

The stitch counts in the pattern panel now match your real-world tension.

## Choose Your Language

AmiLab supports 7 stitch notation systems.

1. Open **Settings → Language**
2. Select your preferred notation: US English, UK English, Deutsch, Nederlands, 日本語, Português, or 中文
3. The pattern panel updates immediately

**Result:** A pattern written in the stitch terms you use.
