# Design a Sphere

Spheres are the foundation of most amigurumi — heads, bodies, and rounded details.

## What You'll Learn

- How to create and shape a sphere
- How symmetry works in AmiLab

## Steps

1. **Create a new project** and select **Sphere**
2. The profile shows a half-circle. Drag control points to flatten or elongate the sphere
3. The **Symmetry** toggle mirrors your edits to the other side of the profile
4. Adjust **Rows** to control how many rounds the pattern generates
5. Open the **Pattern** panel to review the stitch counts
6. **Export** when you're satisfied

**Tip:** A perfect sphere is useful on its own, but you can combine it with other shapes for a full character.
