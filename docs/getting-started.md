# Getting Started

AmiLab runs entirely in your browser — no installation required.

## 1. Open the app

Go to the AmiLab web app and click **New** to create a fresh design.

## 2. Choose a shape type

Use the shape selector to pick a starting point:

| Shape | Best for |
|-------|---------|
| **Sphere** | Heads, bodies, round fruits |
| **Tube** | Arms, legs, tentacles |
| **Custom** | Anything else — draw a freeform profile |

## 3. Set your gauge

Your **gauge** tells AmiLab how many stitches fit in 10 cm with your yarn and hook. Open **Settings → Gauge** and enter your swatch measurement. This makes the stitch count match your actual project size.

## 4. Edit the profile

The **profile editor** shows a cross-section of your shape. Drag the control points to reshape it. The 3D preview updates live on the right.

## 5. Read the pattern

Once you're happy with the shape, the **pattern panel** shows the round-by-round stitch counts. Each row is one round:

```
Round 1:  6 sc in magic ring  (6)
Round 2:  6 inc  (12)
Round 3:  [sc, inc] × 6  (18)
...
```

## 6. Choose your language

Open **Settings → Language** to switch stitch notation. AmiLab supports US English, UK English, German, Dutch, Japanese, Portuguese, and Chinese. See [Crochet Notation](./crochet-notation.md) for a full comparison table.

## 7. Save your design

Click **Save** to store your design in your browser. Sign in with Google to sync across devices.
