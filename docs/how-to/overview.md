# How-to Guides

Solve a specific task in AmiLab. Each guide is a self-contained recipe — find the one that matches what you need to do and follow the steps.

- [Copy your pattern as written instructions](./copy-pattern-as-text.md) — get the row-by-row text out.
- [Build a piece by hand](./build-a-piece-by-hand.md) — design stitch by stitch with increases and decreases.
- [Close up a finished piece](./close-up-a-piece.md) — finish the open end with a gather or seam.
- [Organize your patterns into projects](./organize-patterns-into-projects.md) — load, move, and group saved patterns.
- [Combine pieces into a composition](./combine-pieces-into-a-composition.md) — assemble patterns into one character.
- [Share a composition and its preview](./share-a-composition.md) — publish a public link and set its preview image.
