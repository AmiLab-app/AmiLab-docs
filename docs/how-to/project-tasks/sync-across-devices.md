# Sync Across Devices

Access your AmiLab projects on any device by signing in with Google.

1. Click **Sign In** in the top-right corner
2. Authenticate with your Google account
3. Once signed in, all projects you save are synced to the cloud automatically
4. Open AmiLab on another device, sign in with the same account, and your projects appear in **My Projects**

**Result:** Pick up where you left off, on any device.
