# Adjust Stitches

Fine-tune the stitch count for individual rounds in your pattern.

1. Open your project in the **Editor** workspace
2. In the **Pattern** panel, locate the round you want to change
3. Click the stitch count value to make it editable
4. Enter the new count and press **Enter**
5. The 3D preview and subsequent rounds recalculate automatically

**Result:** The pattern updates to reflect your manual override.
